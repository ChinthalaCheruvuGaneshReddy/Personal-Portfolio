  • Phase 1: Early Years

    During my early years, from kindergarten through 12th grade, I was often perceived as carefree and not particularly serious, mainly due to my academic struggles and lack of guidance. As a child, I was deeply fascinated by games and play, showing little interest in studies. My focus was on having fun, watching TV, and just enjoying myself. Books felt distant and uninteresting. However, this wasn’t solely my fault; the education system and teachers also failed to engage and guide me effectively. Consequently, I struggled academically and was viewed as a dull student.


  • Phase 2: B.Tech Journey

    Entering the B.Tech program at Vignan's Lara Institute of Technology and Science marked a pivotal phase in my life. The initial excitement of college soon gave way to numerous challenges and failures, especially during the second year. The following four years were a roller coaster of fun, failures, and various college experiences. While there were more tough situations than happy moments, I eventually met someone who had a profound impact on me and helped me grow by the end of my course.


  • Phase 3: Post-Graduation Struggles

    After graduation, I faced one of the most challenging periods of my life, marked by a series of failures. My initial plan to pursue a master’s degree in the USA was thwarted by a failed application process and visa rejections. Shifting focus, I decided to upskill by joining Vcube for a Java Full Stack course. This training equipped me with valuable programming skills, allowing me to build a website and an application. Despite these achievements, I faced further setbacks during my visa interviews, but I continued to persevere.


  • Reflection and Resilience

    Every life story has its ups and downs, and mine is no exception. The challenges I faced have been instrumental in shaping my identity and resilience, revealing strengths I didn’t know I had. What I’m most proud of is my ability to face and overcome these challenges. The setbacks I’ve encountered have taught me valuable lessons and pushed me to grow. I now approach future challenges with confidence and the wisdom gained from my past experiences.
